CLONE SYNTHESIS - ARTIST

Clone Synthesis is an all-in experimental electronic arrangement, simulating the atmosphere of synth music. Led by Mitesh Mynee, the project is inspired by the nostalgic melodies of A-ha, David Bowie, Eurythmics and also the new-age sounds of Daft Punk, Dynatron, Lady Gaga and The Weeknd.

CLONE SYNTHESIS - EP

Clone Synthesis is a self-titled debut EP synergizing conscience, emotions and beliefs. The electronic project incepts with a state of soothing, but wild meditation.

EP INSIGHTS

The electronic project incepts with "Genesis", a state of soothing, but wild meditation. A carrier of feelings, "Pollen" is an adventurous track. "Kurōn Gōsei", Japanese translation of Clone Synthesis reveals a progressive touch. While the synth-pop styled "Feral" chronicles the need to adapt, "Osmosis" unveils the delightful melody of triumph and adaptation.
